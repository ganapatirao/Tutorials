Chapter 1: Introduction to Azure

1.1 What is Azure?

Microsoft Azure is a cloud computing platform with more than 200 products and cloud services designed to help you bring new solutions to life, solve today's challenges, and shape the future.

1.2 Azure Regions

Azure has multiple data centers in regions around the world to provide global availability and compliance requirements.

1.3 Azure Subscription Models

  • Pay-As-You-Go: Pay only for what you use
  • Enterprise Agreement: Volume licensing for organizations
  • Free Account: Free credits for first 12 months
